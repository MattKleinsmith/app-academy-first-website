A goal during App Academy

Goal

To create an educational video game.

Why

To increase the meaning of video games.

Backstory and details

Nowadays, I only play video games with friends. Without my friends being there to justify the time spent, it becomes salient to me that I've just honed a skill that is trapped inside a virtual world; I have just gained knowledge that is forever irrelevant from the world I live in.

While outside the scope of an App Academy goal, I do hope to one day create a game I can play with my friends, that hones our relevant skills and gives us relevant knowledge.

The scope of this planned game, however, will be small. It will target a specific, relevant skill.

I do not yet know which skill the game will target, but I will use this project to explore this topic.
